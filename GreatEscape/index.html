What is an Escape Room?

An escape room is a live action adventure that puts you and your friends in the center of the action solving puzzles and or solving a mystery within a set time limit to escape. It's an experience that requires teamwork, and problem solving! Do you have what it takes to escape? Come find out at The Great Escape Killeen!
